After the return of King Richard the Lionheart from Jerusalem and his killing in a raid on French territory before his return to England, Robin Hood assumes the identity of a knight until he returns the crown to the country, then goes to hand over the sword of the knight who assumes his personality to his father, who convinces him to assume the personality of the late son so that he does not inherit the king his lands. The northern barons turn against the king as a result of the betrayal of one of his aides, but Robin Hood leads them to victory over the French invaders before the king turns against him and the well-known legend begins.
